Privacy Policy

Last updated:  ·  Governed by GDPR & CCPA

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how this website collects and handles information when you visit. I am committed to respecting your privacy and being transparent about the minimal data I process.

This site is operated by an individual based in France (European Union) and is subject to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and, where applicable, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

01 — Data Controller

The data controller is the site owner, an individual based in Paris, France. For any privacy-related questions, please use the contact form on this site.

02 — Data Collected & Why

Analytics — Umami Cookie-freeSelf-hosted

This site uses Umami, a privacy-focused, open-source analytics tool, hosted on my own server. No data is sent to any third party. Umami does not use cookies and does not collect personally identifiable information. The data collected includes:

  • Page views and referrer URLs
  • Browser type and operating system (derived from the user agent)
  • Country, region and city of origin (derived from IP address — the IP itself is not stored)
  • Device type (desktop, tablet, mobile)

Legal basis under GDPR: legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f)).

Contact Form Email only

If you use the contact form, your name, email address, and message are transmitted to me by email. This data is:

  • Not stored in any database
  • Used solely to respond to your inquiry
  • Retained only in my email inbox for as long as needed to handle your request

The contact form is protected by Friendly Captcha, a privacy-respecting CAPTCHA service that verifies you are a human without tracking you or using cookies. When you load the contact form, your browser makes a request to Friendly Captcha's servers, which may involve your IP address. Friendly Captcha is GDPR-compliant and does not build user profiles. You can read their privacy policy at friendlycaptcha.com/legal/privacy-end-users.

Legal basis under GDPR: legitimate interest in responding to messages and protecting this site from spam (Article 6(1)(f)).

Server-Side User Agent Logging Temporary

My backend server temporarily logs user agent strings for debugging purposes. This data is:

  • Not stored in any database
  • Not linked to any personal information
  • Automatically discarded when the server process resets

Legal basis under GDPR: legitimate interest in maintaining a functional and secure website (Article 6(1)(f)).

03 — Cookies None

This site does not use cookies of any kind — no analytics cookies, no tracking cookies, no functional cookies.

04 — Third-Party Services

The only third-party service used on this site is Friendly Captcha, used to protect the contact form from spam. No data is shared with advertising networks or data brokers. Umami Analytics is self-hosted and does not involve any third party.

05 — Data Transfers

Friendly Captcha is operated by Friendly Captcha GmbH, based in Germany (EU). Data processed by Friendly Captcha remains within the EEA. No other data is transferred internationally.

06 — Data Retention

  • Analytics (Umami) — retained for statistical purposes; can be deleted upon request
  • Contact form — retained in my email inbox only as long as needed to handle your inquiry
  • User agent logs — not retained; discarded automatically

07 — Your Rights

Under GDPR, if you are in the EU/EEA, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, or restrict your data, to object to processing, and to lodge a complaint with the CNIL (Commission Nationale de l'Informatique et des Libertés).

Under CCPA, if you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information is collected, to request its deletion, and to opt out of its sale. This site does not sell personal information.

To exercise any of your rights, please use the contact form on this site.

08 — Security

This site uses HTTPS to protect data in transit. Given the minimal nature of the data processed, the risk to your rights and freedoms is low.

09 — Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the latest revision. Continued use of the site after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10 — Open Source

The full source code of this website is publicly available on GitHub. You are welcome to inspect it to verify exactly what data is collected, how the contact form works, and how the backend handles requests. Transparency is a core principle of this project.

Note that GitHub itself may collect data when you visit the repository, subject to GitHub's own privacy policy.

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how this website collects and handles information when you visit. I am committed to respecting your privacy and being transparent about the minimal data I process.

This site is operated by an individual based in France (European Union) and is subject to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and, where applicable, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

01 — Data Controller

The data controller is the site owner, an individual based in Tourcoing, France. For any privacy-related questions, please use the contact form on this site.

02 — Data Collected & Why

Analytics — Umami Cookie-free

This site uses Umami, a privacy-focused, open-source analytics tool. Umami does not use cookies and does not collect personally identifiable information. The data collected includes:

  • Page views and referrer URLs
  • Browser type and operating system (derived from the user agent)
  • Country of origin (derived from IP address — the IP itself is not stored)
  • Device type (desktop, tablet, mobile)

Legal basis under GDPR: legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f)).

Contact Form Email only

If you use the contact form, your name, email address, and message are transmitted to me by email. This data is:

  • Not stored in any database
  • Used solely to respond to your inquiry
  • Retained only in my email inbox for as long as needed to handle your request

Legal basis under GDPR: legitimate interest in responding to messages (Article 6(1)(f)).

Server-Side User Agent Logging Temporary

My backend server temporarily logs user agent strings for debugging purposes. This data is:

  • Not stored in any database
  • Not linked to any personal information
  • Automatically discarded when the server process resets

Legal basis under GDPR: legitimate interest in maintaining a functional and secure website (Article 6(1)(f)).

03 — Cookies None

This site does not use cookies of any kind — no analytics cookies, no tracking cookies, no functional cookies.

04 — Third-Party Services

The only third-party service used on this site is Umami Analytics. No data is shared with advertising networks or data brokers.

05 — Data Transfers

If Umami is hosted outside the European Economic Area (EEA), data transfers are subject to appropriate safeguards as required by GDPR Chapter V. No other data is transferred internationally.

06 — Data Retention

  • Analytics (Umami) — retained for statistical purposes; can be deleted upon request
  • Contact form — retained in my email inbox only as long as needed to handle your inquiry
  • User agent logs — not retained; discarded automatically

07 — Your Rights

Under GDPR, if you are in the EU/EEA, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, or restrict your data, to object to processing, and to lodge a complaint with the CNIL (Commission Nationale de l'Informatique et des Libertés).

Under CCPA, if you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information is collected, to request its deletion, and to opt out of its sale. This site does not sell personal information.

To exercise any of your rights, please use the contact form on this site.

08 — Security

This site uses HTTPS to protect data in transit. Given the minimal nature of the data processed, the risk to your rights and freedoms is low.
